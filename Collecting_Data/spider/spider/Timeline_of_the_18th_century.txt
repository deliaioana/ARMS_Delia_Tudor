<li>1701–1714: <a href="/wiki/War_of_the_Spanish_Succession" title="War of the Spanish Succession">War of the Spanish Succession</a> is fought, involving most of continental <a href="/wiki/Louis_XIV_of_France#War_of_the_Spanish_Succession" class="mw-redirect" title="Louis XIV of France">Europe</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>1701–<a href="/wiki/1702" title="1702">1702</a>: <i><a href="/wiki/The_Daily_Courant" title="The Daily Courant">The Daily Courant</a></i> and <i>The Norwich Post</i> become the first daily newspapers in England.</li>
<li>1702: <a href="/wiki/Forty-seven_r%C5%8Dnin" title="Forty-seven rōnin">Forty-seven rōnin</a> attack <a href="/wiki/Kira_Yoshinaka" title="Kira Yoshinaka">Kira Yoshinaka</a> and then commit <a href="/wiki/Seppuku" title="Seppuku">seppuku</a> in Japan.</li>
<li>1702–<a href="/wiki/1715" title="1715">1715</a>: <a href="/wiki/Camisard" class="mw-redirect" title="Camisard">Camisard Rebellion</a> in France.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1703" title="1703">1703</a>: <a href="/wiki/Saint_Petersburg" title="Saint Petersburg">Saint Petersburg</a> is founded by <a href="/wiki/Peter_the_Great" title="Peter the Great">Peter the Great</a>; it is the Russian <a href="/wiki/Capital_(political)" class="mw-redirect" title="Capital (political)">capital</a> until <a href="/wiki/1918" title="1918">1918</a>.</li>
<li>1703–<a href="/wiki/1711" title="1711">1711</a>: The <a href="/wiki/R%C3%A1k%C3%B3czi%27s_War_of_Independence" title="Rákóczi&#39;s War of Independence">Rákóczi Uprising</a> against the <a href="/wiki/Habsburg_monarchy" title="Habsburg monarchy">Habsburg monarchy</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1704" title="1704">1704</a>: End of Japan's <a href="/wiki/Genroku" title="Genroku">Genroku</a> period.</li>
<li>1704: <a href="/wiki/First_Javanese_War_of_Succession" title="First Javanese War of Succession">First Javanese War of Succession</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-RICKLEFSp82_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-RICKLEFSp82-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1705" title="1705">1705</a>: <a href="/wiki/George_Frideric_Handel" title="George Frideric Handel">George Frideric Handel</a>'s first opera, <i><a href="/wiki/Almira" title="Almira">Almira</a></i>, premieres.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1706" title="1706">1706</a>: <a href="/wiki/War_of_the_Spanish_Succession" title="War of the Spanish Succession">War of the Spanish Succession</a>: French troops defeated at the battles of <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Ramillies" title="Battle of Ramillies">Ramillies</a> and <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Turin" class="mw-redirect" title="Battle of Turin">Turin</a>.</li>
<li>1706: The first English-language edition of the <i><a href="/wiki/Arabian_Nights" class="mw-redirect" title="Arabian Nights">Arabian Nights</a></i> is published.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1707" title="1707">1707</a>: The <a href="/wiki/Acts_of_Union_1707" title="Acts of Union 1707">Act of Union</a> is passed, merging the Scottish and English Parliaments, thus establishing the <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Great_Britain" title="Kingdom of Great Britain">Kingdom of Great Britain</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li>1707: After <a href="/wiki/Aurangzeb" title="Aurangzeb">Aurangzeb</a>'s death, the <a href="/wiki/Mughal_Empire" title="Mughal Empire">Mughal Empire</a> enters a long decline and the <a href="/wiki/Maratha_Empire" title="Maratha Empire">Maratha Empire</a> slowly replaces it.</li>
<li>1707: <a href="/wiki/Mount_Fuji" title="Mount Fuji">Mount Fuji</a> erupts in Japan for the first time in 10000 years.</li>
<li>1707: <a href="/wiki/War_of_27_Years" class="mw-redirect" title="War of 27 Years">War of 27 Years</a> between the Marathas and Mughals ends in India.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1708" title="1708">1708</a>: <a href="/wiki/Honourable_East_India_Company" class="mw-redirect" title="Honourable East India Company">The Company of Merchants of London Trading into the East Indies</a> and English Company Trading to the East Indies merge to form the United Company of Merchants of England Trading to the East Indies.</li>
<li>1708–<a href="/wiki/1709" title="1709">1709</a>: Famine kills one-third of <a href="/wiki/East_Prussia" title="East Prussia">East Prussia</a>'s population.</li>
<li>1709: The <a href="/wiki/Great_Frost_of_1709" title="Great Frost of 1709">Great Frost of 1709</a> marks the coldest winter in 500 years.</li>
<li>1709: <a href="/wiki/Hotak_dynasty" title="Hotak dynasty">Hotak dynasty</a> founded in Afghanistan.</li>
<li>1709: <a href="/wiki/Charles_XII_of_Sweden" title="Charles XII of Sweden">Charles XII of Sweden</a> flees to the Ottoman Empire after <a href="/wiki/Peter_I_of_Russia" class="mw-redirect" title="Peter I of Russia">Peter I of Russia</a> defeats his army at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Poltava" title="Battle of Poltava">Battle of Poltava</a>.</li></ul>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="1710s"><a href="/wiki/1710s" title="1710s">1710s</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Timeline_of_the_18th_century&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: 1710s">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/1710" title="1710">1710</a>: The world's first <a href="/wiki/Copyright_law" class="mw-redirect" title="Copyright law">copyright legislation</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Great_Britain" title="Kingdom of Great Britain">Britain</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Statute_of_Anne" title="Statute of Anne">Statute of Anne</a>, takes effect.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1710" title="1710">1710</a>–<a href="/wiki/1711" title="1711">1711</a>: <a href="/wiki/Ottoman_Empire" title="Ottoman Empire">Ottoman Empire</a> fights Russia in the <a href="/wiki/Russo-Turkish_War_(1710%E2%80%931711)" class="mw-redirect" title="Russo-Turkish War (1710–1711)">Russo-Turkish War</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1711" title="1711">1711</a>–<a href="/wiki/1715" title="1715">1715</a>: <a href="/wiki/Tuscarora_War" title="Tuscarora War">Tuscarora War</a> between British, Dutch, and German settlers and the <a href="/wiki/Tuscarora_people" title="Tuscarora people">Tuscarora people</a> of <a href="/wiki/North_Carolina" title="North Carolina">North Carolina</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1712" title="1712">1712</a>: <a href="/wiki/War_of_the_Spanish_Succession" title="War of the Spanish Succession">War of the Spanish Succession</a>: The French defeat a combined Dutch-Austrian force at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Denain" title="Battle of Denain">Battle of Denain</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1712" title="1712">1712</a>: The first shipment of <a href="/wiki/Coffee" title="Coffee">coffee</a> from Java reaches <a href="/wiki/Amsterdam" title="Amsterdam">Amsterdam</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-MOORE90_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MOORE90-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1713" title="1713">1713</a>: The <a href="/wiki/Treaty_of_Utrecht" class="mw-redirect" title="Treaty of Utrecht">Treaty of Utrecht</a> ends the <a href="/wiki/War_of_the_Spanish_Succession" title="War of the Spanish Succession">War of the Spanish Succession</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1713" title="1713">1713</a>–<a href="/wiki/1714" title="1714">1714</a>: <a href="/wiki/Tarabai" title="Tarabai">Tarabai</a> establishes the rival <a href="/wiki/Maratha_Empire" title="Maratha Empire">Maratha Empire</a> government in <a href="/wiki/Kolhapur" title="Kolhapur">Kolhapur</a> against <a href="/wiki/Chattrapati_Shahu" class="mw-redirect" title="Chattrapati Shahu">Chattrapati Shahu</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1714" title="1714">1714</a>: Accession of <a href="/wiki/George_I_of_Great_Britain" title="George I of Great Britain">George I</a>, Elector of Hanover, to the throne of Great Britain.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1715" title="1715">1715</a>: The <a href="/wiki/Jacobite_rising_of_1715" title="Jacobite rising of 1715">first Jacobite rising</a> breaks out; the British halt the Jacobite advance at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Sheriffmuir" title="Battle of Sheriffmuir">Battle of Sheriffmuir</a>; <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Preston_(1715)" title="Battle of Preston (1715)">Battle of Preston</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1715" title="1715">1715</a>: <a href="/wiki/Louis_XIV_of_France" class="mw-redirect" title="Louis XIV of France">Louis XIV</a> dies, leaving France greatly enlarged but deep in debt; <a href="/wiki/R%C3%A9gence" title="Régence">The Regency</a> takes power under <a href="/wiki/Philippe_II,_Duke_of_Orl%C3%A9ans" title="Philippe II, Duke of Orléans">Philippe d'Orleans</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1715" title="1715">1715</a>: <a href="/wiki/Pope_Clement_XI" title="Pope Clement XI">Pope Clement XI</a> <a href="/wiki/Chinese_Rites_controversy#Pope_Clement_XI.27s_decree" title="Chinese Rites controversy">declares</a> <a href="/wiki/Catholicism" class="mw-redirect" title="Catholicism">Catholicism</a> and <a href="/wiki/Confucianism" title="Confucianism">Confucianism</a> incompatible.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1716" title="1716">1716</a>: Establishment of the <a href="/wiki/Sikh_Confederacy" class="mw-redirect" title="Sikh Confederacy">Sikh Confederacy</a> along the present-day India-<a href="/wiki/Pakistan" title="Pakistan">Pakistan</a> border.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1717" title="1717">1717</a>: The Netherlands, Britain and France sign the <a href="/wiki/Triple_Alliance_(1717)" title="Triple Alliance (1717)">Triple Alliance</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1717" title="1717">1717</a>: <a href="/wiki/Surabaya" title="Surabaya">Surabaya</a> rebels against the <a href="/wiki/Dutch_East_India_Company" title="Dutch East India Company">VOC</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-RICKLEFSp84_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-RICKLEFSp84-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1718" title="1718">1718</a>: The city of <a href="/wiki/New_Orleans" title="New Orleans">New Orleans</a> is founded by the French in North America.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1718" title="1718">1718</a>: <a href="/wiki/Blackbeard" title="Blackbeard">Blackbeard</a> (Edward Teach) is killed by <a href="/wiki/Robert_Maynard" title="Robert Maynard">Robert Maynard</a> in a North Carolina inlet on the inner side of <a href="/wiki/Ocracoke_Island" class="mw-redirect" title="Ocracoke Island">Ocracoke Island</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1718" title="1718">1718</a>–<a href="/wiki/1730" title="1730">1730</a>: <a href="/wiki/Tulip_period" title="Tulip period">Tulip period</a> of the Ottoman Empire.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1719" title="1719">1719</a>: The Spanish attempt to restart the <a href="/wiki/Jacobite_rising_of_1719" title="Jacobite rising of 1719">Jacobite rebellion</a> fails.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1719" title="1719">1719</a>: <a href="/wiki/Second_Javanese_War_of_Succession" title="Second Javanese War of Succession">Second Javanese War of Succession</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-RICKLEFSp84_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-RICKLEFSp84-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>

<ul><li><a href="/wiki/1720" title="1720">1720</a>: The <a href="/wiki/South_Sea_Bubble" class="mw-redirect" title="South Sea Bubble">South Sea Bubble</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1720" title="1720">1720</a>: Spanish military embarks on the <a href="/wiki/Villasur_expedition" title="Villasur expedition">Villasur expedition</a>, traveling north from Mexico into the <a href="/wiki/Great_Plains" title="Great Plains">Great Plains</a>.</li>

<li><a href="/wiki/1721" title="1721">1721</a>: <a href="/wiki/Church_reform_of_Peter_I" class="mw-redirect" title="Church reform of Peter I">Peter I reforms</a> the <a href="/wiki/Russian_Orthodox_Church" title="Russian Orthodox Church">Russian Orthodox Church</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1722" title="1722">1722</a>: <a href="/wiki/Pashtun_people" class="mw-redirect" title="Pashtun people">Afghans</a> conquer much of Iran, overthrowing the <a href="/wiki/Safavids" class="mw-redirect" title="Safavids">Safavid</a> <a href="/wiki/Sultan_Husayn" class="mw-redirect" title="Sultan Husayn">Shah Sultan Husayn</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1722" title="1722">1722</a>: Kangxi Emperor of China dies.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1722" title="1722">1722</a>: <a href="/wiki/Bartholomew_Roberts" title="Bartholomew Roberts">Bartholomew Roberts</a> is killed in a sea battle off the African coast.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1722" title="1722">1722</a>–<a href="/wiki/1723" title="1723">1723</a>: <a href="/wiki/Russo-Persian_War_(1722%E2%80%931723)" title="Russo-Persian War (1722–1723)">Russo-Persian War</a>.</li>

<li><a href="/wiki/1724" title="1724">1724</a>: The <a href="/wiki/Treaty_of_Constantinople_(1724)" title="Treaty of Constantinople (1724)">Treaty of Constantinople</a> is signed, partitioning <a href="/wiki/Persia" class="mw-redirect" title="Persia">Persia</a> between the <a href="/wiki/Ottoman_Empire" title="Ottoman Empire">Ottoman Empire</a> and Russia.</li>

<li><a href="/wiki/1726" title="1726">1726</a>: The enormous Chinese encyclopedia <i><a href="/wiki/Gujin_Tushu_Jicheng" title="Gujin Tushu Jicheng">Gujin Tushu Jicheng</a></i> of over 100 million written <a href="/wiki/Chinese_characters" title="Chinese characters">Chinese characters</a> in over 800,000 pages is printed in 60 different copies using <a href="/wiki/Copper" title="Copper">copper</a>-based Chinese <a href="/wiki/Movable_type" title="Movable type">movable type</a> <a href="/wiki/Printing" title="Printing">printing</a>.</li>

<li><a href="/wiki/1727" title="1727">1727</a>–<a href="/wiki/1729" title="1729">1729</a>: <a href="/wiki/Anglo-Spanish_War_(1727%E2%80%931729)" title="Anglo-Spanish War (1727–1729)">Anglo-Spanish War</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1729" title="1729">1729</a>–<a href="/wiki/1735" title="1735">1735</a>: <a href="/wiki/Charles_Wesley" title="Charles Wesley">Charles Wesley</a> and <a href="/wiki/John_Wesley" title="John Wesley">John Wesley</a> begin <a href="/wiki/Methodism" title="Methodism">Methodism</a> in England.</li></ul>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="1790s"><a href="/wiki/1790s" title="1790s">1790s</a></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Timeline_of_the_18th_century&amp;action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: 1790s">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/1790" title="1790">1790s</a>: <a href="/wiki/Canal_Mania" title="Canal Mania">Canal Mania</a> in England and Wales.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1790" title="1790">1790</a>: The <a href="/wiki/United_States_of_Belgium" class="mw-redirect" title="United States of Belgium">United States of Belgium</a> is proclaimed following the <a href="/wiki/Brabant_Revolution" title="Brabant Revolution">Brabant Revolution</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1790" title="1790">1790</a>: Suppression of the <a href="/wiki/United_States_of_Belgium" class="mw-redirect" title="United States of Belgium">United States of Belgium</a> and re-establishment of <a href="/wiki/Austrian_Netherlands" title="Austrian Netherlands">Austrian control</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1790" title="1790">1790</a>: Establishment of the <a href="/wiki/Polish-Prussian_Pact" class="mw-redirect" title="Polish-Prussian Pact">Polish-Prussian Pact</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1791" title="1791">1791</a>: The Constitutional Act (or Canada Act) creates the two provinces of <a href="/wiki/Upper_Canada" title="Upper Canada">Upper</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lower_Canada" title="Lower Canada">Lower Canada</a> in <a href="/wiki/British_North_America" title="British North America">British North America</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1791" title="1791">1791</a>: Suppression of the <a href="/wiki/Li%C3%A8ge_Revolution" title="Liège Revolution">Liège Revolution</a> by <a href="/wiki/Holy_Roman_Empire" title="Holy Roman Empire">Austrian</a> forces and re-establishment of the <a href="/wiki/Prince-Bishopric_of_Li%C3%A8ge" title="Prince-Bishopric of Liège">Prince-Bishopric of Liège</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1791" title="1791">1791</a>–<a href="/wiki/1795" title="1795">1795</a>: <a href="/wiki/George_Vancouver" title="George Vancouver">George Vancouver</a> explores the world during the <a href="/wiki/Vancouver_Expedition" title="Vancouver Expedition">Vancouver Expedition</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1791" title="1791">1791</a>–<a href="/wiki/1804" title="1804">1804</a>: The <a href="/wiki/Haitian_Revolution" title="Haitian Revolution">Haitian Revolution</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1791" title="1791">1791</a>: Surprise Symphony written by <a href="/wiki/Joseph_Haydn" title="Joseph Haydn">Haydn</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1792" title="1792">1792</a>–<a href="/wiki/1802" title="1802">1802</a>: The <a href="/wiki/French_Revolutionary_Wars" title="French Revolutionary Wars">French Revolutionary Wars</a> lead into the <a href="/wiki/Napoleonic_Wars" title="Napoleonic Wars">Napoleonic Wars</a>, which last from <a href="/wiki/1803" title="1803">1803</a>–<a href="/wiki/1815" title="1815">1815</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1792" title="1792">1792</a>: The <a href="/wiki/New_York_Stock_Exchange" title="New York Stock Exchange">New York Stock &amp; Exchange Board</a> is founded.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1792" title="1792">1792</a>: <a href="/wiki/Polish%E2%80%93Russian_War_of_1792" title="Polish–Russian War of 1792">Polish–Russian War of 1792</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1792" title="1792">1792</a>: King <a href="/wiki/Gustav_III_of_Sweden" class="mw-redirect" title="Gustav III of Sweden">Gustav III of Sweden</a> is assassinated by a conspiracy of noblemen.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1792" title="1792">1792</a>: March, <a href="/wiki/Hamengkubuwana_I" class="mw-redirect" title="Hamengkubuwana I">Hamengkubuwana I</a> dies.<sup id="cite_ref-RICKLEFSp101_21-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-RICKLEFSp101-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1793" title="1793">1793</a>: Former King <a href="/wiki/Louis_XVI_of_France" class="mw-redirect" title="Louis XVI of France">Louis XVI of France</a> and his wife <a href="/wiki/Marie_Antoinette" title="Marie Antoinette">Marie Antoinette</a> are <a href="/wiki/Guillotined" class="mw-redirect" title="Guillotined">guillotined</a>. Louis is executed in January, Marie Antoinette in October.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1793" title="1793">1793</a>: <a href="/wiki/Upper_Canada" title="Upper Canada">Upper Canada</a> <a href="/wiki/Act_Against_Slavery" title="Act Against Slavery">bans slavery</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1793" title="1793">1793</a>: The largest <a href="/wiki/1793_Philadelphia_yellow_fever_epidemic" title="1793 Philadelphia yellow fever epidemic">yellow fever epidemic</a> in American history kills as many as 5,000 people in <a href="/wiki/Philadelphia" title="Philadelphia">Philadelphia</a>, roughly 10% of the population.<sup id="cite_ref-22" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1793" title="1793">1793</a>–<a href="/wiki/1796" title="1796">1796</a>: <a href="/wiki/Revolt_in_the_Vend%C3%A9e" class="mw-redirect" title="Revolt in the Vendée">Revolt in the Vendée</a> against the French Republic at the time of the <a href="/wiki/French_Revolution" title="French Revolution">Revolution</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1794" title="1794">1794</a>–<a href="/wiki/1816" title="1816">1816</a>: The <a href="/wiki/Hawkesbury_and_Nepean_Wars" title="Hawkesbury and Nepean Wars">Hawkesbury and Nepean Wars</a>, which were a series of incidents between settlers and <a href="/wiki/New_South_Wales_Corps" title="New South Wales Corps">New South Wales Corps</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Aboriginal_Australian" class="mw-redirect" title="Aboriginal Australian">Aboriginal Australian</a> clans of the <a href="/wiki/Hawkesbury_River" title="Hawkesbury River">Hawkesbury river</a> in <a href="/wiki/Sydney" title="Sydney">Sydney</a>, <a href="/wiki/Australia" title="Australia">Australia</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1794" title="1794">1794</a>: <a href="/wiki/Ko%C5%9Bciuszko_Uprising" title="Kościuszko Uprising">Polish revolt</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1794" title="1794">1794</a>: <a href="/wiki/Jay%27s_Treaty" class="mw-redirect" title="Jay&#39;s Treaty">Jay's Treaty</a> is concluded between Great Britain and the United States, by which the Western outposts in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Lakes" title="Great Lakes">Great Lakes</a> are returned to the U.S. and commerce between the two countries is regulated.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1794" title="1794">1794</a>: <a href="/wiki/Qajar_dynasty" title="Qajar dynasty">Qajar dynasty</a> founded in Iran after replacing the Zand dynasty.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1794" title="1794">1794</a>: <a href="/wiki/The_Mysteries_of_Udolpho" title="The Mysteries of Udolpho">The Mysteries of Udolpho</a> by <a href="/wiki/Ann_Radcliffe" title="Ann Radcliffe">Ann Radcliffe</a> published.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1795" title="1795">1795</a>: <a href="/wiki/Mohammad_Khan_Qajar" class="mw-redirect" title="Mohammad Khan Qajar">Mohammad Khan Qajar</a> razes <a href="/wiki/Tbilisi" title="Tbilisi">Tbilisi</a> to the ground.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1795" title="1795">1795</a>: Establishment of the French-backed <a href="/wiki/Batavian_Republic" title="Batavian Republic">Batavian Republic</a> in present-day Netherlands.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1795" title="1795">1795</a>: <a href="/wiki/Pinckney%27s_Treaty" title="Pinckney&#39;s Treaty">Pinckney's Treaty</a> between the United States and Spain grants the <a href="/wiki/Mississippi_Territory" title="Mississippi Territory">Mississippi Territory</a> to the U.S.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1795" title="1795">1795</a>: The <a href="/wiki/Marseillaise" class="mw-redirect" title="Marseillaise">Marseillaise</a> is officially adopted as the French <a href="/wiki/National_anthem" title="National anthem">national anthem</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1795" title="1795">1795</a>: <a href="/wiki/Kamehameha_I" title="Kamehameha I">Kamehameha I</a> of the Island of <a href="/wiki/Hawaii_(island)" title="Hawaii (island)">Hawaii</a> defeats the <a href="/wiki/Oahu" title="Oahu">Oahuans</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Nu%27uanu" class="mw-redirect" title="Battle of Nu&#39;uanu">Battle of Nu'uanu</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1796" title="1796">1796</a>: <a href="/wiki/Edward_Jenner" title="Edward Jenner">Edward Jenner</a> administers the first <a href="/wiki/Smallpox_vaccination" class="mw-redirect" title="Smallpox vaccination">smallpox vaccination</a>; <a href="/wiki/Smallpox" title="Smallpox">smallpox</a> killed an estimated 400,000 Europeans each year during the 18th century, including five reigning <a href="/wiki/Monarch" title="Monarch">monarchs</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-23" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1796" title="1796">1796</a>: <a href="/wiki/War_of_the_First_Coalition" title="War of the First Coalition">War of the First Coalition</a>: The <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Montenotte" title="Battle of Montenotte">Battle of Montenotte</a> marks <a href="/wiki/Napoleon_Bonaparte" class="mw-redirect" title="Napoleon Bonaparte">Napoleon Bonaparte</a>'s first victory as an army commander.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1796" title="1796">1796</a>: The British eject the Dutch from <a href="/wiki/Ceylon" class="mw-redirect" title="Ceylon">Ceylon</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1796" title="1796">1796</a>: <a href="/wiki/Mungo_Park_(explorer)" title="Mungo Park (explorer)">Mungo Park</a>, backed by the <a href="/wiki/African_Association" title="African Association">African Association</a>, is the first European to set eyes on the <a href="/wiki/Niger_River" title="Niger River">Niger River</a> in Africa.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1796" title="1796">1796</a>–<a href="/wiki/1804" title="1804">1804</a>: The <a href="/wiki/White_Lotus_Rebellion" title="White Lotus Rebellion">White Lotus Rebellion</a> against the <a href="/wiki/Manchu_dynasty" class="mw-redirect" title="Manchu dynasty">Manchu dynasty</a> in China.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1796" title="1796">1796</a>: <a href="/wiki/Trinidad" title="Trinidad">Trinidad</a> put under British rule.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1797" title="1797">1797</a>: <a href="/wiki/John_Adams" title="John Adams">John Adams</a> is inaugurated on March 4 as President of the United States following the <a href="/wiki/1796_United_States_presidential_election" title="1796 United States presidential election">1796 United States presidential election</a>. The peaceful transfer of power from the Washington administration to Adams sets a precedent for relinquishing executive power and transferring it to a new administration.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1797" title="1797">1797</a>: <a href="/wiki/Napoleon_I_of_France" class="mw-redirect" title="Napoleon I of France">Napoleon</a>'s invasion and partition of the <a href="/wiki/Republic_of_Venice" title="Republic of Venice">Republic of Venice</a> ends over 1,000 years of independence for the Serene Republic.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1798" title="1798">1798</a>: The <a href="/wiki/Irish_Rebellion_of_1798" title="Irish Rebellion of 1798">Irish Rebellion</a> fails to overthrow <a href="/wiki/British_rule_in_Ireland" title="British rule in Ireland">British rule in Ireland</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1798" title="1798">1798</a>–<a href="/wiki/1800" title="1800">1800</a>: The <a href="/wiki/Quasi-War" title="Quasi-War">Quasi-War</a> is fought between the United States and France.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1799" title="1799">1799</a>: <a href="/wiki/Napoleon_I_of_France" class="mw-redirect" title="Napoleon I of France">Napoleon</a> stages a <a href="/wiki/Coup_d%27%C3%A9tat" title="Coup d&#39;état">coup d'état</a> and becomes <a href="/wiki/First_Consul" class="mw-redirect" title="First Consul">First Consul</a> of France.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1799" title="1799">1799</a>: <a href="/wiki/Dutch_East_India_Company" title="Dutch East India Company">Dutch East India Company</a> is dissolved.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1799" title="1799">1799</a>: The assassination of the 14th <a href="/wiki/Tu%27i_Kanokupolu" class="mw-redirect" title="Tu&#39;i Kanokupolu">Tu'i Kanokupolu</a>, Tukuʻaho, plunges <a href="/wiki/Tonga" title="Tonga">Tonga</a> into half a century of civil war.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1799" title="1799">1799</a>: <a href="/wiki/Tipu_Sultan" title="Tipu Sultan">Tipu Sultan</a> is killed in a battle with British forces.</li></ul>
